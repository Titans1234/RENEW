Best Places To Visit


Agra

One of the most beautiful monuments in the world, Taj Mahal is located on the banks of River Yamuna in Agra.

Kerala

It's known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals. Inland are the Western Ghats and mountains.

Mumbai

Mumbai has three main seasons — Summer, Monsoon& Winter. The best time to visit is between November and February.

Hyderabad

Its historic sites include Golconda Fort, a former diamond-trading center that was once the Qutb Shahi dynastic capital.

Goa

Goa is best known for its beaches, ranging from popular stretches at Baga,Palolem to those in villages such as Agonda.
